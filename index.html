How to use this app

OK! So this is the first app I’ve ever made, so I’m making my excuses early 😅
(And I’m not going to pretend that ChatGPT didn’t do most of the work!)

For the Shot Watcher

  1. Pick the period duration.
  2. Pick which team is in the goal you’re watching.
  3. Tap Start Game.
  4. Enter the goalie # and tap Start Period.
  5. Use Save/Goal buttons to track shots.
  6. If goalie switches: tap Switch Goalie, enter new # and game clock time, then confirm.
  7. Repeat until period ends → tap Next Period.
  8. After P3 tap End Game.
  9. Don’t press Reset!
  10. Show the tally to the gamesheet keeper ✅
